The heyvm sandbox is a headless ubuntu:24.04 microVM provisioned by `heyvm create` with only filesystem mounts and NO Wayland/DISPLAY forwarding (verified: zero WAYLAND/DISPLAY/Xvfb refs in plugins/heyvm/printer-plugin.toml or config.rs). Conclusion: current behavior is expected/correct for a sandboxed headless review. The fix is environmental.
